HOW TO BE ACTIVE AT HOME DURING THE #COVID-19 OUTBREAK

Looking after yourself , your family and friends is important through these stressful times.

WHO encourages everyone at home to do simple & fun physical activities to stay healthy & active.

Here are some simple physical and mental activities.

1.Keeping active every day, mind and spiritis good for your body especially during these stressful times.

And more physical activities you can do can improve your sleep which is also important for good health.

2.Doing any activity around the home is better than doing nothing at all.

Be active during #COVID-19 outbreak to maintain your heaert health, musucle strength and flexibility. It is good for your mental health too!

3.Regularly check your sitting posture while working from home .

4.Break up your sitting & stand up while working, on the phone , or watching TV
